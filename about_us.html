Our Message

CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

In the pursuit of knowledge through education, there are lot of vital forces that drives the same education. Those forces, at times, stand as a barrier for the people who earnestly deserve it. With a vision to provide technical education, to those who deserve it but being denied on meanless grounds, arises GRACE COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING. Its’ through divine Grace we stand . The personality of an individual is not only determined by his physique but also by his wholistic development. One of our objectives is not only providing knowledge from text books but also imparting various skills-including technical, material, language, social, to one who step into GCOE as a student. The best combination in education is skilled and trained teachers working with students in the pursuit of their learning process. This force us to place qualified scholars to qualify our students for the world filled with competitions and challenges. As Martin Luther king Jr stated : Intelligence plus character, that’s the true goal of education, we value every individuals’ character and career and we strive to enlighten them. It is our foreseen vision that as days-go-by when anyone thinks of engineering education, their mind should first flash as GCOE.

SECRETARY'S MESSAGE

Grace College of Engineering as its name speaks stands on the foundation of divine grace. An individual has miles to go ; if they stop at the milestone of GCOE, the rest of the journey becomes tireless. Knowledge blended with high ethical values are being imbibed into young minds by the well-equipped faculty members. GCOE is a training field as it is loaded with lot of equipments where a student can train to do what they learn in text. GCOE can accommodate students who are faraway from their study environment and provides an in-home atmosphere during their stay at GCOE hostel. We motivate all to fly into sky by supporting them in inculcating various skills of interest. We guide them to the doors of opportunities before them through campus placements.
