ABOUT THE TRUST

Grace College of Engineering

Grace College of Engineering guides students to fulfil their dreams to become full-fledged engineers through effective engineering streams and excellent training. The aspects of our college have been designed in a manner to achieve the objectives of the aspirants and to meet the goals of our management by providing superlative academic standards.

Developing technocrats with excellent conceptual understanding and skills for innovative solutions to societal needs.

Vision

To provide platform for stimulating ideas through technical interactions and industrial exposures.

To impart conceptual understanding through excellent education and practical exposure.

To cultivate managerial and soft skills through leadership development workshops for entrepreneurship development.

To inculcate ethical values and practices to exhibit genuine concern to serve society.

Mission

Excellence and Genuineness for Innovation.
